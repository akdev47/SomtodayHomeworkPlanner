FAQ


General FAQs:

Q1: What is the purpose of this app?
A: This app is designed to help teachers and students manage classes, homework, modules, and other educational activities in an organized and efficient manner.


For Students

Q1: How can I submit homework?
A: Navigate to the specific class, go to the "Assignments" section, click on the relevant assignment, and use the "Submit" button to upload your work.


Q2: Can I communicate with my teachers through the app?
A: Yes, you can contact any teacher through their school email.


Q3: Can i split my homework
A: Yes, go to your assignment that you'd like to split and make a request to split your homework.


For Teachers

Q1: How do I create a new class?
A: After logging in, click on "Create Class," fill in the class details, and add the students to your class.


Q2: How do I upload course materials?
A: Go to the specific class, navigate to the "Assignments" section, and click the add button to upload documents, videos, and other resources.


Q5: Can I track attendance using the app?
A: Yes, you can mark attendance by going to the "Attendance" section in your class dashboard. You can mark students as present, absent, or late.


Q6: How do I create quizzes or tests?
A: Navigate to the "Quizzes" section, click on "Create Quiz," enter the questions and settings, and publish it. Students will be able to take the quiz within the set time frame.


Technical Support

Q1: What should I do if the app is not working properly?
A: First, try refreshing the page or restarting the app. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. You can also contact our support team through the "Help" section.


Q2: How do I report a bug or give feedback?
A: You can report bugs or provide feedback by sending an email to support@sometoday.nl . Describe the issue or your suggestions, and our team will address it promptly.


Q3: Is my data secure on this app?
A: Yes, we use advanced encryption and security protocols to ensure that all your data is safe and secure.


Miscellaneous

Q1: Can I customize the notifications I receive?
A: Yes, you can customize your notifications by going to the "Settings" section and selecting your notification preferences.


Q2: Is there a mobile version of this app?
A: Yes, our app is available on both iOS and Android. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.

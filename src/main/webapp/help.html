FAQ


General FAQs:

Q1: What is the purpose of this app?
A: This app is designed to help teachers and students manage classes, homework, modules, and other educational activities in an organized and efficient manner.


For Students

Q1: What can I do in the dashboard?
A: You can view all of your classes, assignments for those classes, and assignments on the calender.


Q2: Can I communicate with my teachers through the app?
A: Yes, you can contact any teacher through their school email.


Q3: Can I split my homework
A: Yes as a student, you can go to your assignment that you'd like to split and make a request to split your homework.


Q4: How can I view my assignments?
A: Go to the "Assignments" section in your dashboard. Here, you can see all your assignments from your teachers for all classes.


For Teachers

Q1: How do I view my classes?
A: After logging in, click on "Classes," fill in the class details, and add the students to your class.


Q2: How do I change homeworks to be splittable?
A: When you are creating an homework assignment, you can check the checkbox to aet if the homework is splittable or not.


Technical Support

Q1: What should I do if the app is not working properly?
A: First, try refreshing the page or restarting the app. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. You can also contact our support team through our support email somtoday6info@gmail.com.


Q2: How do I report a bug or give feedback?
A: You can report bugs or provide feedback by sending an email to somtoday6info@gmail.com. Describe the issue or your suggestions, and our team will address it promptly.


Q3: Is my data secure on this app?
A: Yes, we use hashing and security protocols to ensure that your data secure.


Q4: What are the system requirements for using this app?
A: The app requires a modern web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari) and an internet connection. For the best experience, ensure your browser is up-to-date.


Miscellaneous

Q1: Is there a mobile version of this app?
A: No, we do not have a mobile version, however you can access our application through an internet browser.


Q2: How can I update my profile information?
A: Go to the "Profile" section, click on "Edit Profile," and update your information such as name, email, and password.
